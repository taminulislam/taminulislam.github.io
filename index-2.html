Biography

Taminul Islam received his Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) with first-class in the field of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) from Daffodil International University, Ashulia, Dhaka, Bangladesh. He worked as an Undergraduate Research Assistant at Daffodil International University during his student life. He was also a student prefect/Undergraduate Teaching Assistant in his student life. After graduating, he started a professional career as a Research and Development Assistant. He achieved a Full Free Scholarship in his B.Sc. program from Daffodil International University.

His research interest lies in the application of Machine Learning and Deep Learning on Healthcare informatics and Disease Prediction, Cybersecurity, and Plants Disease Prediction. He has already published some quality research on cybersecurity, healthcare, and plant disease domain in IEEE, Springer, Taylor & Francis, and ScienceDirect. Currently, he is working as a Researcher in two leading research labs STEMResearch.Ai and AI-Explain You Science (AI-EYS). He reviewed articles for different journal and conferences. He mentored almost 50 students so far in different research projects. Currently, he is collaborating actively with renowned professors and researchers in several other disciplines of Computer Science. He is a member of IEEE.
